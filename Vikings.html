Viking Re-enactment



I take part in Viking re-enactment as part of the Midgardsormr group in Grays, Essex. We train twice a week every week on a monday and a wednesday. I am training to be something called a Scram fighter, this means that I will be on the battle fields in both line fighting and as a sports fighter in circles (or when the lines break up and carnage takes place)

I fight mostly with a Lang-seax (Long Sword), a Scramseax (Knife) and an Axe (...Axe)
My most recent event was at the Jorvik 2018 festival which is the largest viking show in Europe where I fought in both the Day Battle as a line fighter and in the Night Battle which is essentially a scripted all out war that tells the story of the Battle of the Heathen by the light of bonfires and torches!
